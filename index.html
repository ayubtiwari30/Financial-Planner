📱 Install as App on iPhone

To use this as a full-screen app on your iPhone:

  1. Open this file in Safari (not Chrome or Firefox)
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it "Family Planner" and tap Add
  5. Open the app from your Home Screen — it runs full-screen with no browser bar!

ℹ️ After adding to Home Screen, the app works completely offline — no internet needed.
